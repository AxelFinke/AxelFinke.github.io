Profile

I am currently a research fellow working with Alexandre Thiery at the Department of Statistics and Applied Probability, National University of Singapore.

Research interests

I work on methodology for computationally intensive Bayesian and Frequentist statistics. In particular, I am interested in

  • sequential Monte Carlo and Markov chain Monte Carlo methods,
  • analysis of exact and approximate algorithms in high dimensions,
  • applications in epigenetics, ecology, econometrics, finance.
